Vegan Buddha Bowl

A nutritious and colorful Buddha bowl packed with roasted vegetables, grains, and plant-based protein, topped with a creamy tahini dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup fresh spinach or kale
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp tahini

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, sweet potato, chickpeas, spinach, avocado, olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
  3. Spread the mixture in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the sweet potato is tender and the chickpeas are lightly browned.
  4. While the bowl is baking, prepare the tahini dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lime juice, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  5. Once the bowl is done, remove it from the oven and drizzle the tahini dressing over the top.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
