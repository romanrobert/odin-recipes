Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies made entirely vegan, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup coconut oil or vegan butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup vegan chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted coconut oil, brown sugar, white sugar, and almond milk until smooth.
  3. Add the vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared baking sheet and smooth the top.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  7. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
