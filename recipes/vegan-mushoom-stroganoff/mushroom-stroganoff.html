Vegan Mushroom Stroganoff

A comforting and creamy mushroom stroganoff made with plant-based ingredients and served over your favorite pasta.

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta (e.g., fettuccine or penne)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup unsweetened plant-based milk (e.g., almond or oat)
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and start to brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the plant-based milk, nutritional yeast, soy sauce, and cornstarch. Stir until the sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce and toss to coat. Serve hot.
